    KATHY BILL

     

    Born in Montreal, Canada

    Lives & works in Provo, UT

     

    ​

    My practice as an interdisciplinary artist is a quest of reflecting the confrontation of the mind against the body’s limitations and the relationship between physical limits and our sublime experience in the landscape.
